LSJ Launcher Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 5, 2025

1. Data Collection

LSJ Launcher does not collect any personal data. The application only reads files and folders that you explicitly open or drag into the launcher.

2. Data Usage

All data stays locally on your device. The application does not send or share your information over the internet.

3. Third-Party Services

LSJ Launcher does not use analytics, advertisements, or any external tracking services.

4. User Control

You can uninstall the application at any time. All data associated with the app will be deleted with the uninstallation.
